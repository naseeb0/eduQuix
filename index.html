What is this website?

This website is built for both students and teachers alike. There are many features to facilitate learning even during lockdown. Scroll down to see what features we have!

Features

computer

Zoom Meetings

Is it hard to navigate to the Zoom app or you don't have the Zoom app installed? Fret not, with the Zoom app embeded in this website, you can join meetings hassle-free!

School Supplies Delivery

Lacking school supplies but can't go out with so many restrictions? We have a marketplace for you to buy all your school essentials! And don't worry about collection, they will be delivered to your house in no time!

Assignments Submission and Marking

Google Classroom, Edmodo, Blackboard Learn...you name it! So many different places to submit and mark assignments. But with this website, all you need is a camera to scan your work or a text editor to type out your assignment, and viola!

More Features Coming Your Way

With this Beta release, we have so many more features we want to integrate! With your patience, you will see them soon!
